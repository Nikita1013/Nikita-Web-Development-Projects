The Best K-Dramas According to Nikita

My top-3 K-Dramas of all time

Boys Over Flower

This is the best K-Drama based on how the heir to multinational Company falls in love with the Poor Girl in the late 90's

The Heirs

It is also a K-Drama about the Illegitimate heir falling in love with the poor girl but in the trending era.

Doom at Your Service

This drama is about how the Doom saves a Girl who is about to die because of tumor but he saves her and then he himself becomes normal human and lives Happily Ever After.
